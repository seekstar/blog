用```\Statex```使其不占用行号即可。
```tex
\begin{algorithm}
\caption{2333}\label{233}
\begin{algorithmic}[1]
\Statex \textbf{Input:} 233
\Statex \textbf{Output:} 23333
\State 2233
\end{algorithmic}
\end{algorithm}
```
